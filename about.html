ABOUT US

Our Story:

Since opening in 2019, Cafe Smile has been offering delicious coffee, various kind of beverages, pastries, and brunches that make people feel warm and happy. We also provide a warm and safe place where everyone can enjoy their time with friends and put a smile on their face when they leave. Starting from this year, we created a membership system that customers can sign-up for which they can receive reward points and a free special drink on their birthday to show customers our appreciation.

Cafe Smile's mission:
